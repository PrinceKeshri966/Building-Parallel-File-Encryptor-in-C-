About Company

Silicon Labs (Silabs) Inc. is a leader in secure, intelligent wireless technology for a more connected world. We are innovators in the semiconductor space for IoT and related products, with global revenue of $1B (USD). We have also been actively acquiring promising companies and start-ups across the globe. Headquartered in Austin, Texas, with a strong presence in Massachusetts, California, and Florida in the US, Silicon Labs also has a vibrant global footprint in regions including Singapore, India, China, Japan, Australia, Canada, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Italy, Hungary, Norway, Taiwan, South Korea, and the UK.

Job ProfileWhat You Will Do:
Projects are specifically tailored to each intern, ensuring a meaningful contribution to both your skillset and Silicon Labs.
Here are just a few of the challenges you might get to tackle:
** Design and Develop sample applications in IoT Protocol.
** Enabling Matter over Wi-Fi applications on various chipsets.
** Debug and fix issues on the IoT applications.
** Enhance software applications quality by improving code coverage.

Who We Are Looking For:
** Strong understanding of Object-oriented design and proficiency in C++
** Excellent analytical and debugging skills
** Familiarity of Python, TCP/IP Networking is a plus
** Exposure with development tools for embedded systems such as analyzers, JTAG, etc. is a plus.
** Reliable, quick to act, and able to work effectively in a team environment.
